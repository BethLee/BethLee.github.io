Uniquely Rendered by Freehand Machine Embroidery

Each hair is lovingly sewn into place, depicting the direction of your pets fur, the light in the eyes are picked out with glittery and hand sewn embellishments.

Layer upon layer of colour is painted with the thread, to create the finished, almost three dimensional portrait. Capturing the essence of your best friend, more than a photograph, for an eternity.

About

Stacey Chapman has a degree in Illustration and after a background in retail, loved her job designing sets for events and corporate launches. Art Sea Craft Sea. The business came about through making gifts for family members from up-cycled fabrics and the desire to reduce unnecessary landfill.
